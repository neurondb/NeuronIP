## 1. Enterprise Readiness

### Recommendation: Focus on Enterprise Sales Blockers

**Priority:** Critical  
**Timeline:** Q1 2024  
**Investment:** High

#### Key Actions:

1. **Implement SSO (SAML, OAuth, OIDC)**
   - **Why:** Blocks all enterprise sales - 100% of enterprise customers require SSO
   - **Impact:** Unlocks enterprise market
   - **Effort:** 6-8 weeks
   - **ROI:** High - Enables enterprise sales pipeline

2. **Build Multi-Source Data Catalog**
   - **Why:** Enterprise customers have 10-50+ data sources
   - **Impact:** Essential for enterprise adoption
   - **Effort:** 12-16 weeks (connector framework + top 10 connectors)
   - **ROI:** High - Core enterprise requirement

3. **Enhance High Availability**
   - **Why:** Enterprise requires 99.9%+ uptime
   - **Impact:** Enterprise requirement
   - **Effort:** 8-12 weeks
   - **ROI:** Medium-High - Enterprise requirement

4. **Comprehensive Audit Logging**
   - **Why:** Compliance and security requirement
   - **Impact:** Enterprise requirement
   - **Effort:** 4-6 weeks
   - **ROI:** Medium - Compliance requirement

#### Success Metrics:
- ✅ SSO implemented and tested with 3+ identity providers
- ✅ 10+ data source connectors operational
- ✅ HA setup documented with 99.9% uptime target
- ✅ Comprehensive audit logs for all operations

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

1. **Connector Framework Complexity**
   - **Risk:** High complexity, many edge cases
   - **Mitigation:** Start simple, iterate, extensive testing
   - **Contingency:** Partner with connector vendors

2. **SSO Integration Complexity**
   - **Risk:** Many protocols, security-sensitive
   - **Mitigation:** Use proven libraries, security review
   - **Contingency:** Partner with identity providers

3. **Performance at Scale**
   - **Risk:** Lineage visualization, large catalogs
   - **Mitigation:** Performance testing, optimization
   - **Contingency:** Pagination, lazy loading

### Business Risks

1. **Resource Constraints**
   - **Risk:** May not have enough engineers
   - **Mitigation:** Prioritize critical gaps, consider contractors
   - **Contingency:** Extend timeline, reduce scope

2. **Market Changes**
   - **Risk:** Competitors may introduce new features
   - **Mitigation:** Continuous competitive monitoring
   - **Contingency:** Adjust roadmap dynamically
